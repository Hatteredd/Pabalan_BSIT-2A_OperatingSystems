About Windows XP

Windows XP unified Microsoft’s consumer and business operating systems on a single NT-based platform. It introduced the colorful Luna interface, strong driver support, and major improvements to stability and usability. XP became one of the longest-lived and most widely used versions of Windows in history.

The video guide demonstrates how to install Windows XP, walk through the out-of-box experience, and apply initial configuration tasks such as networking and updates. Studying XP gives you insight into why so many organizations and home users relied on it for more than a decade.
